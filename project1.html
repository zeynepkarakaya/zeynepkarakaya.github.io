Lyrixe

Personalized gift unwrapping experience design

Project: Bespoke Design

Duration: 2 months

Team: Liz Yau, Zeynep Karakaya

My Role: Design Partner

Tools: Miro, otter.ai, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD, Adobe Premiere Pro, Zoom

Objective

The project's aim was creating a unique gift unwrapping experience for our specific users. Accordingly, we went through the design thinking process such as empathize, define, ideate, prototype, and test
